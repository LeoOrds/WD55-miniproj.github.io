About This Page

Hi! I am Victor Louis N. Ordonio currently enrolled in Kodego, my project is about the pilgrim churches in the Archdiocese of Lingayen-Dagupan on which I have been serving for almost 10 yaers espicifacally the Parish Of St. john the Baptist in Bautista, Pangasinan, and also in the Sanctuario de San Juan Evangelista (the old Cathedral).
This Parishes or Basilicas, holds the title of PORTA SANCTA or Holy Door
A very important symbolic act performed by each pilgrim has been to pass through the Holy Door. Christ identified Himself as “the door.” In His bull Incarnationis Mysterium proclaiming this Holy Year, Pope John Paul II stated that the Holy Door “…evokes the passage from sin to grace which every Christian is called to accomplish. Jesus said, ‘I am the door’ (John 10:7) in order to make it clear that no one can come to the Father except through Him.
