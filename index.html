Welcome Pilgrim!


These selected churches serve as a guide for people who want to visit the famous churches in the province of Pangasinan. The history of each of these churches has made a great contribution to the area it belongs to.


Most of the people visit sacred places like these church to pay respect and pray. It is one way to communicate in the form of prayers with the help of people believing in the similar beliefs. The location or this churches is perfectly placed for people wanted to find peace since churches at Dagupan is near with the beaches.


Every churches structure defines its owns history behind it. Tourist usually come to visit the outstanding designs and historical events of each churches. The details on the wall preserve the past, present, and future like the stories and lecture written in a book. People give importance to the cultural value as what they give significant to their beliefs. However, there's only one thing that unite us. It's the belief that something is above us, a prayer unites us to be a good disciples of God.
